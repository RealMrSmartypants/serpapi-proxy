1. Fundamentals of Local SEO & AEO
What is local SEO, and why is it a must-have for my business? +

Local SEO is the strategy of optimizing your business's online presence to ensure you show up front and center when nearby customers search for your services. If you serve a specific geographic area or have a physical storefront, local SEO connects you with highly motivated buyers who are ready to purchase right now. Instead of competing globally, you dominate your immediate market.

What is AEO, and why is it the next frontier for local businesses? +

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your business information so AI search engines, voice assistants, and smart devices can instantly pull and deliver your business as the single, definitive answer to a user's question. Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in a list of links. AEO focuses on being the direct answer spoken aloud by a smartphone or displayed in an AI search summary.

Does LocalBoostPro support Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)? +

Yes, absolutely. LocalBoostPro is built for the future of search. The platform utilizes advanced AI content assistants to automatically format your business descriptions, services, and Q&As into conversational, structured data schemas. By feeding AI models and voice assistants the precise, structured data they look for, LocalBoostPro ensures your business is highly visible across both traditional search engines and next-generation AI platforms.

4. Strategic Keyword Engineering
How many keywords should I target? +

For your specialized business profile, focus is power. We recommend selecting about 10 core phrases that represent your highest-value services combined with your location (e.g., “dentist Chicago” or “hairdresser Miami”). The platform tracks these positions daily on a real-time visual grid map so you know exactly where you stand against the local competition.

Why should I focus on location keywords instead of broad terms? +

Generic terms might look attractive because of high nationwide search numbers, but they waste your time and budget because they lack immediate local buyer intent. Furthermore, because modern smart devices rely heavily on automatic GPS geolocation, users frequently skip typing phrases like "near me" entirely. They simply state the service they want, and search engines automatically filter for the closest optimized business. LocalBoostPro targets explicit location-modified keywords and handles structural local optimizations to capture these highly lucrative queries seamlessly.
